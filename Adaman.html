Although you are heir to the throne, the monarch might live a very long time. Your plan to speed things along will only work if the major personalities in the kingdom back your play. You need to use your resources to buy, trick or eliminate anyone who might stand in your way, whether they're in the capital or the Palace!

How to Play

Click on one or more of the cards in the bottom row (Resources) and on one card from the top two rows (the Palace and the Capital) to "control" it. The total value of the selected resources must equal or exceed the value of the card you want to control, and at least one of the suits must match. (The unnumbered cards are Crowns, which count as 10, and Aces, which count as 1.)

"Personality" cards, the Decktet face cards, are highlighted with an orange shadow. When you control them, they are discarded and you score points. When you control non-personality cards, they are moved to your resources. The resources you used to control a card are always discarded.

All holes in the Capital or Resources are filled in from the deck, but Personalities cannot be Resources; they go to the Palace instead. Don't let more than 5 Personalities collect in the Palace, or together they will defeat you. You also lose if you run out of moves before eliminating all eleven Personalities.

The rules are also in the Decktet Wiki. For advice, see the Adaman strategy forum at BoardGameGeek.
