Although you are heir to the throne, the monarch might live a very long time. Your plan to speed things along will only work if the major personalities in the kingdom back your play.

You need to use your resources to buy, trick or eliminate anyone who might stand in your way whether they're in the city or the Palace!

How to Play

You use the cards in the bottom row (resources) to eliminate cards in the top two rows (the Palace and the City), by matching the value of the target card. You can only select cards that match one or more of the suits on the target card. Click to select, click again to deselect a card.

You score points for eliminating "personality cards". Don't let too many (5) "personality" cards collect in the Palace, or together they will defeat you.
